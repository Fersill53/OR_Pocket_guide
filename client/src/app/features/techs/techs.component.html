Back Table Setup

Visual and step-by-step guidance for standard back table layouts.

  1. Open and organize basins, towels, and drapes.
  2. Separate instruments by type: cutting, grasping, retracting, etc.
  3. Line up clamps and hemostats from small to large.
  4. Keep sharp items in a designated “sharps zone”.
  5. Reserve a clean zone for implants / grafts if needed.

Later you can add diagrams or photos (e.g. ortho, laparoscopy, neuro sets) plus procedure-specific layout variations.

Mayo Setup

Common mayo stand layouts to support the surgeon’s first 10–15 minutes.

  • Top tier: knife, pickups, hemostats, scissors, needle holder.
  • Middle: most frequently used instruments for that procedure.
  • Edge: frequently used sutures and Bovie tip within easy reach.
  • Keep a “flip zone” open for when you add extra instruments.
